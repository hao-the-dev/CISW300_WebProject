Featured event

August 2025

Healthcare Pathways Presentation

Hosted by Jasraj · APAPA Youth Leadership Academy

This event marked the introduction of Via Health to Sacramento’s middle school and high school AAPI population through the APAPA Youth Leadership Academy.

During this session, we introduced healthcare pathway frameworks, recruited our first cohort, and assessed student feedback to better understand how Via Health could respond accurately to our audience’s needs over time.

Insights from this event helped shape Via Health’s long-term direction and gave us an early foundation for building something responsive, practical, and community-grounded.
