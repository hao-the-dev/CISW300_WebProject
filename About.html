What is Via Health?

Via Health helps students navigate healthcare pathways by providing decision-focused mentorship—connecting them to people with relevant, recent experience when it matters most.

Who is this for?

We’re currently focused on high school and college-aged California students as the initial mentorship network is being developed. In the future, the platform may expand to support students in other regions as the network grows.

Where we’re starting

Built with California students in mind.

Via Health is especially interested in supporting students in California, including those navigating healthcare pathways without easy access to built-in mentorship.

California Northstate University (CNU) is located in Elk Grove, near the Sacramento region.
